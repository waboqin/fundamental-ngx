Selector: <fd-calendar>

Months: 1 = January, 12 = december. Days: 1 = Sunday, 7 = Saturday

Calendar component used for selecting dates, typically used by the DatePicker and DateTimePicker components. Supports the Angular forms module, enabling form validity, ngModel, etc.
